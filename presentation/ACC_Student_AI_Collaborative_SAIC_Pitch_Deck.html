Level 0: Observer

Anyone welcome, no registration

  • Attend 1 event
  • "Try AI" workshops

Level 1: Explorer

ACC Student, any major

  • Register via ACC email
  • Choose track (Tech/Creative/Business/Health)
  • Free tool access (Foundation tier)
